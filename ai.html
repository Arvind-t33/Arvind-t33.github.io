What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is defined as the theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages.

Components of Artificial Intelligence:

A repeated trial and error process where the algorithms keep track of the actions that led to the desired results and constantly update them.

The ability to draw conculsions or inferences from a situation. Reasoning also allows the AI to maintain consistent results given the same scenario multiple times.

Coming up with efficient algorithms to solve a given problem through the manipulation of variables and other problem-solving techniques.

Using various methods to percieve given inputs and categorize or identify them as certain objects that they are familiar with (have been exposed to them during training).

Using the above defined components to come up with the best set of rules to define different languages that they are exposed to, giving them the ability to understand more than just the English language.
